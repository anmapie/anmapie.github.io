I am full-stack web developer specializing in beautiful, simple, and intuitive tools and interfaces that delight and empower users. Currently, I work for Bonanza.com, an online marketplace specializing in everything but the ordinary. My primary work is done in Ruby on Rails, HTML5, Sass, JavaScript, jQuery, and React; in the past I have dabbled in Python, C#, and Java. I have degrees in Computer Science, Gender and Women's Studies, and Communication Arts, allowing me to bring a unique perspective to design and development.

I believe in mobile-first development, Clarke's third law, and tutorial-free websites.

Sole developer of Bonanza's Customer Marketing Tool, a unique feature allowing sellers to accumulate, analyze, and organize data from both buyers and browsers. They can use this data to craft targeted marketing campaigns. Since it was deployed in the summer of 2016, our sellers have created over 7700 marketing campaigns, generating over $94,000 in sales. The Customer Marketing Tool continues to be used in site promotions and special events (such as Black Friday) and is used in our marketing materials to differentiate us from competitors. Additionally, it has been featured twice on our own blog (a data-focused post and a campaign-focused post) as well as on eCommerce Bytes.

Developed a content management app to allow non-developers to quickly implement clean and responsive shopping landing pages with custom banners, images, and links. These pages have proven to be valuable revenue drivers for Bonanza, increasing revenue up to 150% in key categories over a variety of holidays and occasions. View some examples of it in action at Bonanza's Summer Home shop and Halloween shop.

Completely rebuilt a redesigned version of Bonanza's most important page: the item listing page. The new version is modular, easy to update and rearrange, and, most importantly, fully responsive. Unlike the previous version of the page, it looks good on all screen sizes. This no doubt contributed to the 35% increase we saw in mobile conversion in the quarter following the deployment of the new design. Visit one of Bonanza's listings to see it in action.

Implemented an expansion and redesign of Bonanza's seller stats pages, including richly detailed graphs and the ability to dive deep into metrics for individual items, categories, and advertising outlets. Worked with a design spec and the Google Charts API to create graphs that were both beautiful and informative. Sellers consistently rely on this page to keep them informed and help them make important decisions about their business. In addition, I wrote the blog post announcing the new page.
